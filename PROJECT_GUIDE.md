# PROJECT CEO

## Vision

Project CEO is a business simulation game.

The player starts after losing their job with €20,000 in savings.

The goal is not to become rich as fast as possible.

The goal is to build a resilient business that survives crises and grows through good decisions.

---

## Core Principles

- Every system must create meaningful decisions.
- Money is a consequence, not the objective.
- No pay-to-win.
- Simplicity over unnecessary complexity.
- Modular architecture.
- Every feature must have a purpose.
- The world evolves independently from the player.
- The player should learn economics naturally through gameplay.

---

## Coding Rules

- Keep scripts small.
- Separate systems.
- Write readable code.
- Comment only when useful.
- Never rewrite unrelated systems.
